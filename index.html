Our Vision

At PEGISUS, we are dedicated to addressing critical challenges faced by young people in Zambia, Zimbabwe, and South Africa. We believe that by fostering healthy choices and promoting gender equity, we can unlock their full potential.

Focus on Youth

Over 60% of Africa's population are young people, making their well-being central to the continent's future.

Holistic Approach

We tackle substance use, gender beliefs, and employment opportunities to create lasting impact.

Regional Impact

Our work spans Zambia, Zimbabwe, and South Africa, fostering healthier communities.
